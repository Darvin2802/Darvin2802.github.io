Asset Intelligence · Multi-agent System
Renewable Asset Portfolio Research Automation

What I Built

A multi-agent LLM research system assigning 7 specialist roles — Planner, Website Miner, Filings Analyst, Regulator Miner, Extractor, QA Auditor, Publisher — each with defined responsibilities and strict anti-hallucination guardrails.

Key Design Decisions

  • Source hierarchy enforcement (Company site → SEC → Regulator → Media)
  • 11 defined QA flag types surfacing data quality issues automatically
  • Two research rounds before any field is marked incomplete
  • Every output cell hyperlinked to its primary source

PM Signal

Decomposed a complex research process into roles, rules, and failure modes — the same thinking required to spec a product feature.

Scope

US & Canada renewable assets: Solar, Wind, BESS, Hybrid, Hydro. Covers operational, under construction, development, planned, and retired assets across SPVs, JVs, and subsidiaries.

Document Intelligence · Two-Pass Extraction
M&A Transaction Extraction from Reports & Filings
Analysts used manual Ctrl+F keyword search across SEC filings, industry reports, and press releases to flag M&A deals. At 1–2 hours per document, with deals buried in footnotes and exhibits routinely missed, the process couldn't keep up with deal volume.

What I Built

A structured extraction prompt that processes any document type — SEC/SEDAR filings, trade reports, press releases — and extracts every transaction with full context, sourcing, and analyst-grade commentary via a 6-step chain-of-thought reasoning sequence.

Key Design Decisions

  • 80+ keyword primary list + second-pass expanded set for financing language missed by Ctrl+F
  • Historical transaction capture (completed, pending, ongoing)
  • Cross-check routine auditing high-yield sections after initial pass
  • Structured narrative output — ready for analyst use, no reformatting

PM Signal

Two-pass architecture with a cross-check routine shows deliberate design against failure modes. Deep domain knowledge encoded into a reusable system — exactly what a domain PM does when speccing a feature.

Coverage

Acquisitions, mergers, divestitures, JVs, investments, partnerships, LOI/MOU/LOA steps, options, earn-ins, royalties, licenses, tenders, financing agreements, hedge buyouts, and equity contributions.

What I Built

A real-time transaction intelligence prompt monitoring Business Wire, PR Newswire, GlobeNewswire, and Newswire.ca simultaneously, applying rigorous inclusion/exclusion logic and delivering a structured, deduplicated deal table daily.

Key Design Decisions

  • Two-layer signal detection: headline + body text, not headline alone
  • 20+ transaction types included; operational noise explicitly excluded
  • Cross-platform, cross-language deduplication
  • Pagination enforcement — no pages skipped
  • Three-section output with full coverage transparency

PM Signal

Identified a task consuming disproportionate team capacity and replaced it with a system — not just a faster way of doing the same thing. Eliminated a single point of failure the team didn't realize they had.

Impact Beyond Time

Freed a junior analyst from a repetitive daily task entirely, redirecting that capacity toward higher-value analysis. Parameterized date range means one prompt covers daily, weekly, or retroactive research.
