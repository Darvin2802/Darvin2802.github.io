## AI Workflow Case Studies
 
### 01 — Renewable Asset Portfolio Research Automation
**Problem:** Team manually searched 6+ regulatory databases per company — 3–4 hours per research, inconsistent citations, risk in M&A due diligence.
 
**Solution:** Multi-agent LLM system with 7 specialist roles (Planner, Website Miner, Filings Analyst, Regulator Miner, Extractor, QA Auditor, Publisher), strict anti-hallucination guardrails, 11 QA flag types, and full source citation on every output cell.
 
**Impact:** ~75% time saved · 30–60 min per company · Cross-team adoption
 
---
 
### 02 — M&A Transaction Extraction from Reports & Filings
**Problem:** Analysts used Ctrl+F keyword search across SEC filings and trade reports — deals buried in footnotes routinely missed, 1–2 hours per document.
 
**Solution:** Two-pass extraction system with 80+ keyword primary list + expanded financing-language pass. 6-step chain-of-thought per transaction. Cross-check routine auditing high-yield sections after initial pass.
 
**Impact:** ~80% time saved · 15–20 min per report · All document types covered
 
---
 
### 03 — Real-Time M&A News Monitoring Engine
**Problem:** A junior analyst spent 2–3 hours daily manually scanning 5 press release platforms — single point of failure, inconsistent coverage.
 
**Solution:** Multi-platform monitoring prompt covering Business Wire, PR Newswire, GlobeNewswire, Newswire.ca. Two-layer signal detection (headline + body), cross-platform deduplication, pagination enforcement, 3-section structured output.
 
**Impact:** ~85% time saved · Daily team dependency · 5 platforms covered simultaneously
 
---
 
### 04 — M&A Deal Memo Intelligence System
**Problem:** Analysts wrote deal memos from scratch each time — 2–4 hours per deal, quality varied by seniority, material documents routinely missed.
 
**Solution:** 10-step mandatory research workflow encoding senior analyst judgment into a reusable LLM system. Controlled 12-type transaction vocabulary, ±7 day presentation search window, document priority hierarchy, 15 anti-hallucination rules, ownership chain mapping, legal term simplification, full source register on every output.
 
**Impact:** ~60% time saved · 60–90 min per memo · Junior analyst output raised to senior standard
